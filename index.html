About Us

At the Africa Hope Center, we offer a one of a kind guesthouse and conference facility nestled in a tranquil, natural garden atmosphere.

We are located just five minutes outside of Narok Town ,half way between Nairobi and Maasai Mara. Affordable rates ,a convenient yet private location, and 24-hour security make the Africa Hope Center the perfect place to rest in the middle of a long journey or to host your next group gathering or meeting.
